Our Restaurant

Treat yourself to a gastronomic adventure when you dine at pine & dine..

With a variety of delicacies from the world over to choose from, we strive to give you the best possible dining experience
when you head to the restaurants at our resorts! Staffed with experienced chefs who go the extra mile to tickle your taste buds,
your dining outing is sure to be one to remember. The exquisitely designed restaurants & bars, the ambience, the freshly-prepared
aromatic meals, and courteous staff to look after you, are but some of the things in place to give you a wholesome, enjoyable, and
ultimately memorable experience.
